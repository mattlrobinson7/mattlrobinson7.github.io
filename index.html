Date Locate

Started as a Facebook Seattle hackathon project and grew into a side project me and a friend worked on for about 6 months. If someone has a date lined up, but no idea where to go or what do do, they could use our site and find ideas from other people and see what worked best. NCAA Tournament Smart Bracket Generator

During one spring break, I decided to create a bracket generator that would correctly predict the outcomes at the NCAA Basketball Tournament in hopes of winning the $1billion prize for having a perfect bracket. Used data from the last 25 years of NCAA tournaments and on average, my brackets were in the 52.65th percentile which means on average, my brackets beat over half of the people who made their own.
